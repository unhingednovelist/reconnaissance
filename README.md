# reconnaissance
The universe has gone quiet. Metallic skeletons slumber underneath red dust storms. Upon the desolate surface of Mars in the far-distant future, the lonely corpses of human-sent robotic rovers spark to life, and are left to pick up the pieces of their uninhabited solar system.

*Reconnaissance* is an experimental fiction narrative presented in a digital multimodal format that explores separation, togetherness, and the human condition through the lens of inhuman characters.
